Subhash Chandra Bose

Date of Birth: 23 January 1897

Date of Death: 18 August 1945

About:

Subhash Chandra Bose was a prominent freedom fighter and the leader of the Indian National Army. He is known for his militant approach to Indian independence and his efforts to gain international support for India's freedom.

Contribution to Indian Independence:

Bose's aggressive approach and formation of the Indian National Army played a significant role in the fight for independence, inspiring many with his slogan "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom!".
