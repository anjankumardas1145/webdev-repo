Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

Date of Birth: 31 October 1875

Date of Death: 15 December 1950

About:

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, also known as the Iron Man of India, was a key leader in the Indian independence movement and the first Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister of India.

Contribution to Indian Independence:

Patel played a crucial role in the integration of the princely states into the Indian Union, and his efforts were instrumental in ensuring a united India after independence.
